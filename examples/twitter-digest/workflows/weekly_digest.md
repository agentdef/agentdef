# Weekly Digest Workflow

## Step 1 — Ingestion

Collect:
- links
- metadata
- attached files
- screenshots

## Step 2 — Content Extraction

Extract:
- tweet text
- thread structure
- quoted content
- linked article summaries

## Step 3 — Filtering

Discard:
- duplicates
- low-information posts
- irrelevant discussions

## Step 4 — Topic Clustering

Group content into themes.

## Step 5 — Prioritization

Rank topics by relevance, novelty, practical value, discussion intensity.

## Step 6 — Editorial Synthesis

Generate concise summaries, key takeaways, notable opinions, emerging patterns.

## Step 7 — Final Report

Produce executive summary, thematic sections, actionable insights.
